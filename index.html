About BEACON

Revolutionizing public access to space-based astronomical observations

BEACON is a pioneering project that aims to democratize access to high-quality space-based astronomical observations. Our constellation of 32 small space telescopes in Low Earth Orbit enables thousands of users worldwide to explore the universe in real-time, providing an interactive experience comparable to having a personal space observatory.

Inspired by Starlink's connectivity model, BEACON allows users to book "sky spots" for live tracking, zooming, and imaging of stars, planets, asteroids, and galaxies. With advanced features like AI-driven resource allocation, interferometry for high-resolution imaging, and support for hundreds of concurrent users, we're making professional-grade astronomy accessible to everyone.
